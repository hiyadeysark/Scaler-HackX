About Us

FinP is a Financial Planning tool which can be used for real life financial analysis as well as for learning financial planning. We at FinP believe that all whether a student or a working professional in any field should have knowledge about Financial planning . Our aim is to provide technical and practical knowledge about how to handle finances.

Step 1: New? Create and account

Sign up or login to experience FinP. Enter all your details, you can edit them in the future.

Step 2: Set your Wallet

The wallet limit and saving per month have to be set while signing up and also can be changes from the profile settings. You can go crazy on how much you want in your wallet :). But we suggest to be as real as possible for a better learning experience.

Step 3: Add your expenses.

The monthy expenses like food, shopping, travel, etc can be added and the expenditure can be tracked down. Make sure to keep updating your expenditure.
